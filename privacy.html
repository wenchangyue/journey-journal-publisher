Journey Journal Publisher Privacy Policy

Journey Journal Publisher is a personal publishing tool used by the account owner to upload street-walk short videos to TikTok.

The tool uses TikTok OAuth only to authorize the account owner and upload videos that the account owner selects.

The tool does not collect, sell, share, or store personal information from third-party users.

OAuth credentials and access tokens are stored locally by the account owner and are not published in this repository.
